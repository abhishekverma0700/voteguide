Election Timeline

Follow the step-by-step process of an election cycle.

What: Citizens register to vote.

Who: Election office / citizens.

Deadline: Typically weeks before voting day.

What: Candidates submit nominations and paperwork.

Who: Political parties and independent candidates.

Deadline: Fixed by election schedule.

What: Parties/ candidates present platforms.

Who: Candidates, parties, media.

Note: Model Code of Conduct applies from announcement.

What: Voters cast their ballots at polling stations.

Who: Registered voters, polling staff.

Date: Announced by the election commission.

What: Votes are tallied and verified.

Who: Election officials, observers.

When: Immediately after polling closes.

What: Final outcomes are published.

Who: Election commission.

After: Certification and possible challenges.
